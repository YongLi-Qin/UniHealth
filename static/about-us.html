About Our Team

  • Uni Health is an app that can record one's health data.Our aim is to help the users manage their diabetes better by providing individualised advice from professional medical clinicians.
  • Diabetes is prevailing and affecting various populations across all ages and this app is developed by a young team with an aim of optimising health outcomes for every diabetic patient in the community.

About this Application

  • This app allows our users to record their health data presents the health data to clinicians in a concise manner; and deliver live medical advice to their patients.
  • These features are only accessible after logging into the app and it is time to join us and be proactive in taking care of yourself!
